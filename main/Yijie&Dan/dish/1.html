Autumn Harvest Bowl

Autumn Harvest Bowl

A warm, comforting bowl that celebrates roasted squash and seasonal produce. Balanced with nutty quinoa and a maple-tahini dressing.

Ingredients
  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled & cubed
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed & cooked
  • 2 cups mixed greens (arugula/spinach)
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds, toasted
  • 2 tbsp tahini, 1 tbsp maple syrup, lemon to taste

Tip: Roast squash with smoked paprika for a deeper flavor.

Citrus Chicken Salad

Citrus Chicken Salad

Bright and light: grilled chicken with a citrus vinaigrette, mixed greens, and toasted almonds for crunch.

Ingredients
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Orange segments and lemon zest
  • Toasted almonds
  • Olive oil + citrus vinaigrette

Tip: Marinate the chicken in a little orange juice for 20 minutes before grilling.

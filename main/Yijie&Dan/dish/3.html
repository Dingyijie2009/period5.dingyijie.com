Grilled Salmon Bento

Grilled Salmon Bento

Perfectly grilled salmon glazed with teriyaki, served with steamed rice and pickled vegetables — a balanced, protein-forward bento.

Ingredients
  • Salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • Steamed white rice
  • Pickled cucumber and carrot
  • Sesame seeds

Tip: Pat the salmon dry and sear skin-side down first for crispy skin.

Spicy Miso Ramen

Spicy Miso Ramen

A steaming bowl of umami-rich miso broth, chewy noodles, and a chili kick — topped with soft egg and scallions.

Ingredients
  • Ramen noodles
  • 2 tbsp white miso, 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Chili oil to taste
  • Soft-boiled egg and sliced scallions

Tip: Toast the miso briefly in a dry pan to deepen the flavor before adding stock.

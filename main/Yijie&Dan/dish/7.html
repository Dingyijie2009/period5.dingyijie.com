Autumn Harvest Bowl

Image placeholder — Autumn Harvest Bowl

A warm, comforting bowl with roasted squash, quinoa, toasted seeds, and a maple-tahini dressing. Ready in 30 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1 small butternut squash, roasted
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • Handful of arugula
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds, toasted
  • Maple-tahini dressing

Tip: Swap quinoa for farro for a nuttier texture.

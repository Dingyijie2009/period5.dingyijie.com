Veggie Tacos

Crispy tortillas filled with spiced roasted vegetables, black beans, and fresh pico — bright, crunchy, and filling.

Ingredients
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Roasted bell peppers & zucchini
  • Black beans
  • Pico de gallo & lime
  • Crumbled cotija or feta

Tip: Warm tortillas over an open flame for extra char and pliability.

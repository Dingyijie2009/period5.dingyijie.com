Margherita Flatbread

Margherita Flatbread

A simple flatbread topped with San Marzano-style tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil — classic and satisfying.

Ingredients
  • Flatbread or pizza dough
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, salt

Tip: Add a drizzle of good olive oil after baking for extra aroma.

Port of Orillia Events

Spend the weekend at the Port of Orillia and check out the latest in cottage, boat and outdoor lifestyle!

Celebrate the arrival of summer in the "Sunshine City"! The Port of Orillia will host a variety of vendors show casing a wide range of products, including watercraft and accessories, cottage, and outdoor products.

There will also be food, artisans, and unique retail vendors. The Port of Orillia is steps from the City's vibrant downtown and Couching Beach Park so bring the whole family and make a day of it.

The Port of Orillia is located on the sunny shore of Lake Couching, a few steps from historic downtown Orillia. The Port is a transient marina and popular stop for boaters travelling the Trent-Severn Waterway.

Adjacent to the Port is Couching Beach Park, which boasts a sandy swimming area, playgrounds, and walking trails.
